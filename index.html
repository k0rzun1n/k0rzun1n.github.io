Playcanvas Shooter
Live demo (open "Editor", choose the first scene and press the Launch button inside)

Added a bunch of features to a raw First Person Shooter template:

  • Limited ammo in the magazine, with unlimited reloads (R button).
  • UI for the ammo.
  • UI for the score.
  • HP bar to enemies.
  • Fixed a bug that didn't allow to jump when walking on top of an enemy.
  • Respawn enemies after death, to a random position on the level.
  • Enemy respawn and a score increment if they fall off the level.
  • Player respawn if they fall off the level.
  • A platform that throws the player Unreal Tournament style.
  • Changed shooting from pistol mode (mouse click), to automatic (mouse down), so bullet is shot every 50ms while mouse is down.
